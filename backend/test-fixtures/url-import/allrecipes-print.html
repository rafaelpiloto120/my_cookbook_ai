World's Best Lasagna

Total Time: 3 hrs 15 mins
Servings: 12

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 12 lasagna noodles

Directions

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic until browned.
  2. Stir in tomatoes, sauce, paste, and seasonings and simmer.
  3. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, sauce, and Parmesan.
  4. Bake until browned and let rest before serving.
